FAQ

CAN I CONTINUE MY OWN WEIGHT TRAINING REGIMEN?

It is advised that athletes only complete Upper-Body and Core work while on the Vert Code Elite -OR- Vert Code Body Weight. We provide an Upper Body guide within the videos tab that describes how to incorporate upper body training that even includes exercise ideas.

WHERE ARE YOU LOCATED

Currently the 'RYSE Dojo' is only located in Bankstown Sydney NSW. However stay tuned as we are looking to expand to different suburbs.

WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN LIFETIME VS. MONTHLY PURCHASE?

The difference between the two is simply in terms of access. Both programs will release progressively/monthly, however, if you purchase the monthly subscription and cancel for any reason, you will no longer have access to the program. If you choose to purchase the lifetime option, you will have access to the program indefinitely and will never have to worry about losing access or logged data.
